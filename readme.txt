This github contains the work that done by Group B to redesign the OSI-CAN website for the ENSE 271 major project.

Documents for First Meeting folder contains the earliest parts of our work with our low-fi prototypes, empathy map, and other similar conceptual work. 

Documents for Second Meeting folder contains our hi-fi prototype and relevant documents.

Documents for Third Meeting contains the documents relevant to our in-progress reprot on November 5th, 2025.
